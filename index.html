Rentmate – Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Rentmate account and associated personal data, please send an email from your registered email address using the details below:

Email: rentmatecare@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

For security reasons, account deletion requests will only be processed if sent from the registered email address linked to the account.

Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 7 working days after verification.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Messages and activity data

Data Retention Policy:

Some information may be retained if required by law or for fraud prevention and legal compliance.
