Rentmate – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Rentmate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information automatically. The only data we process is information you voluntarily send via email for account deletion or support.

2. Use of Information

Any information you send via email is used only to process your request or respond to support queries. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

3. Account Deletion

To request account deletion, email rentmatecare@gmail.com with your registered email or phone number. Requests are processed within 7 working days.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information you voluntarily provide but cannot guarantee absolute security of emails sent to us.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, email us at rentmatecare@gmail.com.
